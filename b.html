Cella

Industrial Design/2013

Cella, came from the concept of “cell”, is a automatic household-planting device combines future technique and living habits.

What is the trend of living in the future? What are the reasons and expectations people having plants at home?

Effiecient, smart, and decorative planting devices are demanded by urban people.Cella has an automatic irrigation system, which is able to manipulate the growth conditions of plants. Users can also use the LED light to provide the insufficient sunlight needs.
