Cambodia Architecture Workshop

Workshop/2013

Through the tactile process of attaching components and defining forms, I often gained insights that helped produce better designs and user experiences.

This skill became useful when I participated in an architecture project in Siem Reap, Cambodia. My teammates and I had to use traditional designs and local materials, and as a further challenge, we had scarce access to tools and electricity.
