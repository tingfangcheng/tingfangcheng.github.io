Bisceted Seeds

Industrial Design/2013

Curated the showcase of Industrial Design Department final showcase. Bisected Seed, traveled through wind, came back to home and bloomed with colors and inspirations.

In this semester we traveled to cities outside Taipei and brought back the elements we found, applied in an Art Installation, a furniture, and household devices.

As a curator, I created a space with furniture and painted white, leading the guests to connect the differeint theme and personalities that each of the students show.
