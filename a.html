Blend

Industrial Design/2015

Blended lines could be found in waves, contour, and ancient pattern. Even more, when we pick up a pen and sketch randomly, we follow this principle unconsciously.

We sketch those blended lines to reach the final shape we had in mind.Before an idea is raised; before a shape is defined; before a decision is made, everything changes little by little.

Integrate the concept of the gradual change of lines, light, and shape into lighting design. The user will be able to see the various appearance of the light in different distance and angle.
