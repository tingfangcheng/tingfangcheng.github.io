Bike Project with ITRI

Industrial Design/2014

In the summer of 2014, I participated in the bicycle design project in ITRI as an intern. ITRI is basically in the business of Letre ,CNC machine ,and manufacture robots research and design.

I worked with the Mechanical Lathe Department to design a new bicycle frame, which can be assembled packed easily by users.
